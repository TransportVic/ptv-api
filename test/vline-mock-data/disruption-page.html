3 Jul 2024
Tweet

Line suspension - Seymour line

UPDATE: 15:45

Services are expected to resume following a freight train breakdown in Kilmore East.

From Southern Cross
The 14:36 Southern Cross - Seymour service terminated at Craigieburn.
The 15:33 Southern Cross - Seymour service will now run through to Seymour. 

To Southern Cross
The 12:51 Albury - Southern Cross service will run through to Southern Cross.
The 16:00 Shepparton - Southern Cross service will run thorugh to Southern Cross. 
The 15:45 Seymour - Southern Cross service will not run today. Passengers are to board replacement coaches. 

General information

  • In Melbourne, coaches will arrive and depart from the Southern Cross Station coach terminal located on Spencer Street, opposite Little Bourke Street.

  • No pets allowed on replacement coaches.

  • There is limited space for bikes on V/Line trains and coach replacement services.

  • Customers needing special assistance please call 1800 800 007 before travelling.

  • For the latest up-to-date information, download the V/Line app, follow us on Twitter @vline_bendigo or speak to a V/Line team member.
